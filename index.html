Why SummitSync?

Most ski trip planning tools are either resort marketing pages or outdated forum posts. SummitSync scores 60 western resorts across four dimensions — snow quality, value, crowds, and access — using real data, not sponsored content.

Every resort gets a score from 0–100 in each category. The algorithm weights snow and value highest because those are what matter most when you're actually there.

No ads. No affiliate deals. No resort partnerships. Just the mountain.
